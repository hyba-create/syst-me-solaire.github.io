Jupiter

Characteristics

Name: Jupiter

Position: 5th planet in the solar system, after the asteroid belt.

Status: Gas giant. Dense atmosphere primarily composed of hydrogen and helium.

Other Information: Jupiter is the largest planet in the solar system. It has a Great Red Spot, a gigantic storm. Jupiter has colorful cloud bands and more than 75 known moons, including the four large Galilean moons.

Characteristics: Largest planet in the solar system. Dense and complex atmosphere. Numerous moons.

Interesting Fact: A day on Jupiter lasts about 10 Earth hours due to its rapid rotation.
