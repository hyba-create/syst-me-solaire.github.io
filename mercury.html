Mercure

Characteristics

Name: Mercury

Position: 1st planet in the solar system, closest to the Sun.

Status: Terrestrial (rocky) planet. No significant atmosphere.

Other Information: Mercury is a small planet with temperatures that vary greatly between day and night due to its lack of atmosphere. A day on Mercury lasts 58.6 Earth days. Its surface is marked by craters and fractures.

Characteristics: Closest to the Sun. No significant atmosphere. Very hot during the day, very cold at night.

Interesting Fact: Its rotation is very slow; a day on Mercury lasts about 58.6 Earth days.
