Venus

Characteristics

Name: Venus

Position: 2nd planet in the solar system, between Mercury and Earth.

Status: Terrestrial (rocky) planet. Thick atmosphere mainly composed of carbon dioxide, with clouds of sulfuric acid.

Other Information: Venus is often called Earth's "sister planet" due to their similar size and composition. It has a runaway greenhouse effect, making it the hottest planet in the solar system. Its surface features volcanoes, mountains, and vast plains.

Characteristics: Thick, toxic atmosphere. Extremely high surface temperatures. Volcanically active.

Interesting Fact: A day on Venus is longer than a year on Venus; it takes about 243 Earth days to rotate once on its axis, and about 225 Earth days to orbit the Sun.
