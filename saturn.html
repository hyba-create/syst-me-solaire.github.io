Saturn

Characteristics

Name: Saturn

Position: 6th planet in the solar system, known for its spectacular ring system.

Status: Gas giant. Atmosphere primarily composed of hydrogen and helium.

Other Information: Saturn is famous for its extensive ring system, which is made of ice and rock particles. It has over 80 known moons, with Titan being the largest and one of the most interesting due to its thick atmosphere and liquid hydrocarbon lakes.
