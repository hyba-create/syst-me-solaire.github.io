Uranus

Characteristics

Name: Uranus

Position: 7th planet in the solar system, third-largest in diameter.

Status: Gas giant. Atmosphere primarily composed of hydrogen, helium, and methane.

Other Information: Uranus is unique in that it rotates on its side, possibly due to a massive collision in its past. This causes extreme seasons lasting over 20 years. Uranus has a faint ring system and 27 known moons, with Titania and Oberon being the largest.

Characteristics: Tilted axis of rotation, causing extreme seasonal variations. Methane in the atmosphere gives it a blue-green color.

Interesting Fact: A day on Uranus lasts about 17.25 Earth hours, but its axial tilt means each pole gets around 42 years of continuous sunlight, followed by 42 years of darkness.
