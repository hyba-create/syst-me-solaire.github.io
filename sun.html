Sun

Characteristics

Name: Sun

Position: Center of the solar system.

Status: G-type main-sequence star (Yellow Dwarf).

Other Information: The Sun is a massive ball of hot plasma primarily composed of hydrogen and helium. It accounts for about 99.86% of the total mass of the solar system. The Sun's core is the site of nuclear fusion, which produces its energy and emits light and heat essential for life on Earth.

Characteristics: Central star of the solar system. Source of light and heat. Composed mainly of hydrogen and helium.

Interesting Fact: The Sun's diameter is about 1.39 million kilometers (864,000 miles), and it has a surface temperature of approximately 5,500 degrees Celsius (9,932 degrees Fahrenheit).
